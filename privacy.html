Privacy Policy

This Age of Makers Game Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) explains Salesforce.com’s (“Salesforce” or “Our”) privacy practices for the Age of Makers Game (the “Game”), an interactive curriculum supporting curated STEAM programs by Educators. The Game is intended for teen students 13 years old and above. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully because it is a binding agreement between You and Salesforce. Please also review the Age of Makers Game Terms of Service (“Terms”), which is incorporated herein, and available here.

Capitalized terms have the definitions set forth in the Terms.

Salesforce does not collect, use, or disclose any information pertaining to a User (“personal data”) through, from or relating to the Game. As explained below, other than anonymized data, all data, including personal data, from the Game will only be stored locally on a User’s computer.
